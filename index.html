BOOK AN APPOINTMENT

AppointmentThe appointment booking feature allows patients to easily schedule appointments with their chosen doctors through the "docConnect" platform. Patients can browse through a list of available doctors, view their schedules, and select a convenient date and time for their appointment. Once the appointment is confirmed, patients receive a notification via email or SMS with the appointment details, including the doctor's name, date, time, and location (if it's an in-person appointment). Patients can also reschedule or cancel their appointments through the platform if necessary.
